Policies

General Science Olympiad Rules

All participants are expected to follow Science Olympiad Rules

  • Each division C team can have a maximumn of 7 seniors and each Divison B team can have a maximumn of 5 9th grade students.
  • Each team must be registered with their State Science Olympiad to participate.
  • Each team may have as many alternates as they wish, but the alternates may only participate in Trial Events.

RSOI Policies

These policies are also on the event modifications document

  • This year, we will have normal length tests!
  • All note sheets (events that require 8.5 * 11-inch piece(s) of paper) must be printed out. However, online binders will be allowed.
  • For note sheets, teams are allowed to have a page with different information for each competitor. Online binders need to be in the same document.
  • SATELLITE ONLY - Event Supervisors will keep track of "time out of browser". If a team spends more than 30 seconds, they will be tiered.
  • Events that are supposed to have stations will give all questions at the same time to be completed within 50 minutes.
  • Participants may use any method of communication such as video call, voice chat, or the built-in Scilympiad chat.
  • All events are 50 minutes long unless event modifications state differently. No-shows will result in n+1 points.
  • Events follow national rules.
  • The final team score will not be influenced by trial events
