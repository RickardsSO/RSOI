About

The Rickards Science Olympiad Invitational, RSOI, was started in 2020 by Sina Mollaei and Dr. Hall with the mission of hosting an early competition accessable to all during the pandemic season. The first Rickards Invitational featured 45 division B teams and 67 division C teams. Last year we had 94 division B teams and 93 division C teams! Our competition is ran by Rickards officers and volunteers from around the country.

This season, RSOI is hosting our competition in both Mini and Satellite formats tournament in February 2023, as well as two In-Person tournaments: one for Division B hosted at Rio Hondo College and another for Division C hosted at Temple City High School, both taking place in January 2023.
