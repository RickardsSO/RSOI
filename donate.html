Event Sponsors

We are looking for people or companies to provide special awards to students that place at our tournament. Most people or companies choose to provide monetary or cash awards to students, however, product awards will also be welcome. You may see event availability here, more details on each high school event here, and more details on each middle school event here

With $200+ you will be able to sponsor first place of an available event of your choosing.

  • You or your company name mentioned at the awards ceremony for the specific event
  • Your name or Company logo on the sponsors page
  • Your name or company logo on the specific test(s) you choose to sponsor

With $500+ you may choose the sponsor more than just first place and choose the specific breakdown.

  • All the benefits above
  • Your company name engraved on the back of medals

Scholarship Sponsors

With $500+ You may give a scholarship to a competitor, team, or school with whatever criteria or form you want.

  • Personalized section in the awards ceremony where you can introduce yourself or your company before we announce the scholarship recipients.
