Tournament Info

November 4-11

Format

We will be having both a Mini and a Satellite competition for division B and C.
Per the SIONIC website, those who wish to attend the Satellite competition must take the tests under the supervision of their coach and those attending the Mini competition must have all members be in remote areas. Our tournament is asynchronous, meaning teams can participate in the 50 minute events anytime during the competition (Nov 4-11).

Events

Since our competition is so early, all build events will be trial events and all test and build events will be test only. We will also be including many different fun trial events and we will be updating the event slate as we get more information

Events

  • High Quality Tests that are not only challenging but also enjoyable to take. Our event supervisors are all competitiors and alumni that have preformed exeptionally at the state and national level
  • Fun Events. One of our main goals is for competitiors to enjoy our competition and being an early season competition, we also want competitors to be able to bond with their team, therefore, we have trial events such as Anagrams, Anything Goes, Geoguessr, etc.
  • An Enjoyable Experience. We want students to have fun while minimizing the workload for coaches. We prioritize prompt email responses within 24 hours and strive for clear, easily comprehensible documents. Mailing list members will receive timely announcements for better coordination.
