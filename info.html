General Info

Tournament date - November 2-9

Format

We will be hosting a one week Satellite competition for division B and C that will take place on the first week of November.

Per the SOINC website, those who wish to attend the Satellite competition must take the tests under the supervision of their coach. Students may take tests anywhere as long as all members taking the tests are present and under a coach's supervision. Our tournament is asynchronous, meaning teams can participate in the 50 minute events anytime during the competition. Students on the team but not actively taking tests do not need to all be at their test-taking location. The tournament starts Nov. 2 at 8:00 AM EDT and ends on Nov. 9 at 11:00 PM EST. We encourage teams from the same school to take tests for a certain event at the same time to ensure academic honesty.

Awards

Custom Rickards Invitational medals will be awarded to the top 15 teams for each division.
Different departments at Florida State University are sponsoring certain division C events, and will award $50-$100 to the students that win the specific events.
